POS payment solution.

You might think of point-of-sale (PoS) systems as nothing more than cash registers, but they're becoming considerably more sophisticated.

They can connect to cellular devices and cloud services, as well as meet consumers' software and hardware needs. You can also integrate them using back-end billing systems and payment processors. ALTFIN provides POS agents in specific locations to help make withdrawals, transfers, deposits and other services more accessible to our users. Many small and medium-sized businesses are turning to cloud-based POS services to eliminate the need for many physical back-end servers.

Altfin card integrated solution

Card payment solutions do more than collect payments for what you're selling when you integrate them into your retail, product, or service organization.

Having an integrated ALTFIN card payment system in place for your business may help you simplify processes and provide excellent customer service, among other things. Integrated card payments make it possible for the laborious, time-consuming accounting and payment processes to function in sync

An ALTFIN card integrated payment solution, by automating payment processing, enables our merchants to take card payments easily inside their existing software; this reduces data entering time and minimizes discrepancies.

Blockchain Technology

Blockchains are best known for their crucial role in cryptocurrency systems, such as Bitcoin, for maintaining a secure and decentralized record of transactions. The innovation with altfin blockchain is that it guarantees the fidelity and security of a record or data and this helps to generate trust without the need for a trusted third party.

The goal of blockchain is to allow digital information to be recorded and distributed, but not edited. In this way, blockchain is the foundation for immutable ledgers, or records of transactions that cannot be altered, deleted, or destroyed. This is why blockchains are also known as a distributed ledger technology (DLT).

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is altfin About?

    AltFin is a payment gateway that is driven to integrate finance with technology to provide new or improved services to businesses and consumers in Nigeria and from all across the globe.

  • How can i make use of this payment gateway?

    The entire project tech team has been actively and progressively working to push the entire altfin infrastructure to the global market, all it takes to use this payment gateway is to go through a KYC procedure to authenticate and validate your account, other informations, will be released in respect to the outcomes of the working developers.

  • Is there a partnership program for investors?

    Yes, altfin is currently an open project for investors, internal and external partners.

  • How secure is altfin?

    All financial data is encrypted and saved in accordance with PCIDSS Level 1 requirements. PCIDSS Level 1 compliance is a set of rules established by credit card businesses and independently audited. In the electronic payment processing business, it is the highest possible rating

    Yes, the altfin payment gateway api with full api documentation will be made available to developers who want to intergrate the system into their project.
