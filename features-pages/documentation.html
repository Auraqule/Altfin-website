About Us

ALTFIN Limited is a client-focused, results-driven financial technology and digital payment system provider that offers proven, trusted, and broad-based financial technology services at a price that won't break the bank. We offer a wide range of cryptocurrency and digital payment services and special discounted rates to startups, nonprofits, cooperatives, and small social enterprises who transact with cryptocurrency and other digital payment platforms. All of our clients receive high-quality professional financial technology services at the local, state, national, and international levels. We assist in the resolution of two fundamental issues: the elimination of cash transactions and the provision of financial services.

Our Mission

To offer services that will integrate finance with technology in order to provide new or improved services to businesses and consumers in Nigeria and from all across the globe.

Our Vision

To disrupt incumbents in the finance industry by expanding financial inclusion and using technology to cut down on operational costs.

Payment service.

A payment service provider, or PSP, provides merchants with the tools they need to accept electronic payments such as credit/debit cards and digital wallets. As a payment service provider, ALTFIN helps companies and low-income individuals accept a variety of online payment methods, including e-wallets, online banking, debit cards, and so on. We make certain that consumer transactions get from point A to point B safely and securely.

Digital banking.

Digital banking refers to the digitization of all aspects of banking, from the front-end to the back-end. This means that digital banks use artificial intelligence software to automate back-end activities including administrative tasks and data processing, relieving personnel of the burden of completing day-to-day tasks. With this online banking service, we help eliminate checks, pay-ins, and demand drafts, among other things. ALTFIN’s digital banking process includes a high degree of automated procedures and online services that allow users to obtain financial information through mobile, PC, and ATM.

Peer-to-peer lending.

If you’re looking for a way to borrow money without having to use the traditional means, think ALTFIN! Peer-to-peer (P2P) lending platforms, like ALTFIN, help to link borrowers with investors without involving the banks. Investors sign up to become lenders in order to receive a higher return than they would from savings accounts, term deposits, and other assets. This function essentially lets you borrow money from other people without going via a financial institution, and at generally lower rates.
